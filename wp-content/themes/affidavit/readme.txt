== Description ==
Affadavit is a theme designed specifically with law firms in mind. Out of the box it supports the Meta Slider, Better Contact Details, and Team Members plugins out of the box so you can include an SEO optimized home page slider as well as incorporating your key contact details and profiling your team members in a clear and attractive way. You can also use the Shortcodes Ultimate plugin to add interactive page content and layouts to any page or post. It is perfect for small law firms or independent attorneys who want a free and easy way to present a professional and contemporary website to prospects and clients.

== Theme Setup ==
* For the contact details, download Better Contact Details and add your info
* For Homepage Sections, create 7 pages for 7 sections(Section 1 to Section 7) and also setup Meta Slider then insert the shortcode for the banner area.

== Notes ==
* nested comments are limited. It can only take 2 levels deep
